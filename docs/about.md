# About FIRST South Carolina

**FIRST South Carolina (FSC)** is the official regional organization supporting *FIRST* Robotics Competition (FRC) teams across the state. Our mission is to raise the technical floor of all teams by sharing knowledge, resources, and code. 

Whether you are a rookie team trying to make your robot drive, or a veteran team implementing NPU-accelerated AprilTag detection, the FSC community is here to support you.

---

## How to Get Help

Don't stay stuck on a bug for days! The best way to get help is to reach out to the community. We have a dedicated network of students, mentors, and CSAs (Control System Advisors) ready to assist.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :simple-discord: **FSC Discord Server**
    
    ---
    
    The fastest way to get help. Jump into the `#programming` channel to ask questions, share code snippets, or debug issues live with other teams. [Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/8FJqZDBqmy)
    
-   :material-web: **Official FSC Website**
    
    ---
    
    Find official event schedules, grant information, and additional team resources hosted by FIRST South Carolina.
    
     [Visit firstsouthcarolina.org](https://www.firstsouthcarolina.org/)

-   :material-forum: **Chief Delphi**
    
    ---
    
    For highly technical deep-dives (like custom UDP protocols or custom vision models), check out the global FRC forum. Many of our FSC teams post whitepapers here. [Visit Chief Delphi](https://www.chiefdelphi.com/)

</div>

!!! tip "Asking Good Questions"
    When asking for help in the Discord, always include:
    
    1. A link to your code.
    2. A description of what you *expect* to happen.
    3. A description of what is *actually* happening (with videos or error logs!).
